Element details

Average atomic masses are standard classroom-style values, and the heaviest synthetic elements are shown with bracketed mass numbers because stable average atomic masses are not typically listed for them.

Units and conventions

  • Freezing point and boiling point are in Kelvin (K).
  • Density is in g/cm³ for solids and liquids, and g/L for gases at 25 °C.
  • Ionization energy and electron affinity are in kJ/mol.
  • Oxidation states are dimensionless integers; listed values are commonly tabulated states.
  • Phase at 25 °C refers to the standard state near 298 K and 1 bar.
  • Presentation mode enlarges text and uses a light background for projectors.
